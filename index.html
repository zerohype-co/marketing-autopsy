// The Problem

Most founders are solving the wrong problem.

01

They add channels instead of fixing the core

LinkedIn isn't working so they try cold email. Cold email doesn't work so they try paid ads. The channel isn't the problem. The message and the targeting are.

02

They hire before they have a system

A marketing hire without a clear ICP, a working channel, and a defined process is just expensive experimentation. With someone else's time.

03

They mistake activity for progress

Posting content, sending sequences, running ads. All of it can feel like momentum while the pipeline stays flat. Busy is not the same as effective.

04

They don't know what to fix first

There are five things broken. They're all connected. Without knowing the order of priority, every fix creates a new problem somewhere else.
